Measuring Privacy Protection

Deng · 1 min

Face images are biometric data (GDPR). The project builds a pipeline that anonymises faces while preserving expression. My task is to quantify how well the anonymiser actually protects identity — by training a strong face recognition model and running the anonymised output back through it.

Problem · ArcFace alone fails

m=0.5 from epoch 1 on 105 classes → train acc 0 %, val acc plateaus at 42 %. The angular margin penalty prevents the head from ever predicting correctly.

Fix · Two-phase schedule

  • Phase 1 (ep 1–5): CE warmup, label smoothing 0.1
  • Phase 2 (ep 6–30): ArcFace, m=0.3
  • Differential LR · cosine anneal · 24.6 M params
