## Training Issues Found & Fixed
- Quick test (20 IDs, 5 epochs): 90.65% test accuracy — GOOD
- Full training (105 IDs, 30 epochs): 0% train acc, 42.89% val acc — PROBLEM
- Root cause: ArcFace margin=0.5 too aggressive, prevents convergence on 105 classes
- Fix applied: Two-phase training (CE warmup 5 epochs → ArcFace with reduced margin 0.3)
- Also added: differential LR (backbone 0.1x), label smoothing 0.1, correct accuracy metric during ArcFace training
- User needs to re-run full training with fixed code
